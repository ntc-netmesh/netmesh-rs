About Us

What is NetMesh?

This project proposal aims to support Memorandum Circular (MC) 07-08-2015 of the National Telecommunications Commission (NTC) that addresses the “Rules on the Measurement of Fixed Broadband/Internet Access Service”. MC 07-08-2015 aims to monitor and measure network parameters of internet quality from the perspective of a customer

What tests are conducted?

Our tests are based on RFC 6349. RFC 6349 describes a practical methodology for measuring end- to-end TCP Throughput in a managed IP network. The goal is to provide a better indication in regard to user experience. In this framework, TCP and IP parameters are specified to optimize TCP Throughput.

How can I help?

You can help our cause by becoming a volunteer! Simply go to Netmesh Web Speedtest or download the Netmesh App in the Google Play Store to contribute!
